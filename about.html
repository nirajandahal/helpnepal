Our Story

Rooted in Compassion, Driven by Action

Help Nepal Himalayan Foundation (HNHF) was established in 2082 BS (2025/26 AD) in response to the urgent needs of vulnerable communities living in the geographically challenging regions of Okhaldhunga district. Founded by a group of dedicated individuals who witnessed firsthand the struggles faced by orphaned children, differently-abled individuals, and impoverished families, HNHF was born out of a simple yet powerful belief: everyone deserves access to education, healthcare, shelter, and opportunity.

Based in Molung Rural Municipality, Ward-6, we work directly with local communities to design programs that create lasting, sustainable change. Our approach is rooted in dignity, respect, and partnership—we don't just provide aid; we empower people to build better futures for themselves and their families.

Since our inception, we have supported hundreds of children, families, and individuals through education scholarships, healthcare camps, disaster relief efforts, and livelihood training programs. Every initiative we launch is informed by the voices and needs of the communities we serve.

Registered with the Government of Nepal

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive support—including education, healthcare, shelter, food, and livelihood training—to the most vulnerable populations in rural Nepal, with a focus on orphaned and differently-abled children, chronically ill individuals, and landless poor families. We are committed to creating sustainable, community-led programs that restore dignity and unlock opportunity.

Our Vision

A future where every child in rural Nepal has access to quality education and healthcare, where vulnerable individuals live with dignity and security, and where communities are empowered with the skills and resources to thrive independently. We envision a Nepal where geography and circumstance are no longer barriers to opportunity.

What We Do

Our Focus Areas

Four comprehensive programs designed to address the deepest needs of rural Nepal's most vulnerable populations.

Children's Education & Care

We provide comprehensive support for orphaned, poor, and differently-abled children, ensuring they have access to the resources they need to grow, learn, and thrive.

  • Full education sponsorship including tuition, books, uniforms, and school supplies
  • Healthcare services, regular medical check-ups, and medication support
  • Nutritious meals, safe boarding facilities, and recreational activities
  • Specialized support for children with disabilities including therapy and assistive devices

Support for Vulnerable Individuals

We extend a helping hand to chronically ill individuals, homeless people, and single women and men who have no family support network.

  • Regular food and clothing distribution to those in need
  • Access to medical care for chronic illness management
  • Shelter assistance and housing support for homeless individuals
  • Emotional and social support to combat isolation

Agriculture & Livelihood Training

We empower capable youth and landless poor families with the skills and resources they need to build sustainable, independent livelihoods through agriculture.

  • Hands-on farming training in sustainable agriculture practices
  • Seed distribution and access to farming tools and equipment
  • Soil management, crop rotation, and organic farming techniques
  • Market linkages to help farmers sell their produce and generate income

Disaster Relief & Emergency Response

When natural disasters strike, we mobilize quickly to provide emergency relief and support to affected communities in the Okhaldhunga region.

  • Rapid deployment of food, water, and emergency supplies
  • Temporary shelter and safe housing for displaced families
  • Medical aid and trauma support for those affected by disasters
  • Long-term recovery support to help communities rebuild
