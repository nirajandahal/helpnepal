Projects That Transform Lives

From empowering individuals with disabilities to building sustainable livelihoods — every initiative is rooted in compassion and community.

What We Do

Programs Built Around People

HNHF runs targeted programs across education, health, disability support, and sustainable agriculture — each designed to create lasting change in Nepal's remote regions.

Disability Support

Empowerment of Persons with Physical & Mental Disabilities

We design and deliver inclusive programs that empower individuals living with physical and mental disabilities — giving them access to resources, skills, and community participation so they can lead dignified, self-sufficient lives.

Community Care

Support for Chronically Ill, the Poor & Single Parents

Chronic illness, poverty and single-parent households are deeply interlinked in rural Nepal. Our program channels healthcare access, financial literacy, and emotional support to these vulnerable groups — empowering them to rebuild stability.

Child Welfare

Education, Health & Shelter for Orphaned & Disabled Children

We ensure that poor, orphaned, and physically disabled school-age children receive not only education and healthcare but also the basic necessities — food, shelter, and clothing — so no child's potential goes unrealised.

Livelihood

Agriculture Livelihood for Youth & Landless Women & Men

Capable youth and landless women and men deserve a fair path to self-sufficiency. Through agricultural training, micro-land support, and market linkage, we turn potential into productive, sustainable livelihoods.
