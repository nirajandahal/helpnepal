What We Do

Our Core Pillars

Four fundamental programs designed to address the deepest needs of rural Nepal's most vulnerable populations.

Children's Education & Care

Comprehensive support including education, healthcare, nutrition, shelter, and boarding for orphaned, poor, and differently-abled children.

Vulnerable Individual Support

Food, clothing, and essential care for chronically ill, homeless, and single women and men with no family support network.

Agriculture & Livelihood

Practical farming knowledge and hands-on training for capable youth and landless poor to build sustainable, independent livelihoods.

Disaster Relief

Rapid emergency response during natural disasters—providing food, water, temporary shelter, and medical aid to affected local communities.

Your Support Changes Lives

Every contribution directly provides education, healthcare, food, shelter, and hope to vulnerable communities in rural Nepal.
