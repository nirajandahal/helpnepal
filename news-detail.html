Education 28 January 2025

50 Children Enrolment in Molung Primary School

Thanks to the generous support of our donors and the active participation of the local community, 50 children from vulnerable families in Molung Ward-6 have been fully enrolled in Molung Primary School for the 2025–26 academic year.

Each child has been provided with a complete school uniform, a full set of textbooks, stationery, and a guaranteed mid-day meal throughout the year. This initiative removes two of the biggest reasons families in remote areas withdraw their children from school — the cost of supplies and the lack of nutrition during the school day.

The enrolment drive was coordinated over three weeks by HNHF field staff in close collaboration with the school's principal and the Ward-6 local government office. Families were identified through our existing child-welfare database, and each household was visited individually to confirm eligibility and gather documentation.

"This is not just about books," said HNHF Programme Officer Lakshmi Gurung. "When a child feels looked after — fed, clothed, and supported — they actually stay in the classroom. That is where real change begins."

Beyond the immediate enrolment, HNHF has also arranged quarterly progress reviews with the school. Teachers will flag any child at risk of dropping out early, and our team will step in with targeted support before it reaches that point.

We are grateful to every donor who made this possible. If you'd like to sponsor a child's education for a full year, head to our donation page — even a small contribution makes a measurable difference.
